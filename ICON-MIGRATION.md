## Build Script Change

`scripts/build.js` — the colour validator was updated to also allow `none`, `white`, `#fff`, and `#ffffff` fill/stroke values. This is needed for brand icons that use white as part of their multi-colour design (e.g. the white "f" inside the Facebook icon).
